Do you have Questions?

Here are the most frequently asked question about our plugin.

What version can I use?

Our plugin will work on Minecraft 1.8, 1.9, 1.10, 1.11 and 1.12 server versions. When new Minecraft versions will appear, we'll update our plugin.

Is this plugin free?

Yes, this plugin is free for lifetime, and it will remain like this forever. Anyway, any donation will be appreciated, because it really motivate us and show us your appreciation for what we're doing.

We plan to add a paid extension of the plugin, but the most of the features will remain free. Who said a good plugin needs to be a paid one?

We want to make sure you'll enjoy our plugin. That is the reason for we're asking you to don't give a bad review if you don't get an answer in maximum 48 hours.
